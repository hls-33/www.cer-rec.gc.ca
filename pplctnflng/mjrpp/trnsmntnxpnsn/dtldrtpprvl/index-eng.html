Regulatory Documents

Regulatory documents for this project: [Folder 2392873]

Trans Mountain has filed its Plan, Profile, and Book of Reference (PPBoR) for the entire proposed Trans Mountain Expansion Project (Project) route [Folder 3808418] . The PPBoR has been filed by segment (1-7), which shows the proposed detailed route, as well as the landowners and occupants for over 2500 tracts of land required for the Project. These tracts are grouped on nearly 400 PPBoR sheets, which each require approval of the Commission of the CER (Commission).  Construction can only proceed on lands where the detailed route has been approved, and applicable conditions have been met.

The July 19, 2019, decision on resuming regulatory processes [Filing C00593] , directed Trans Mountain to issue notices to landowners along the entire route. Trans Mountain did so in a staggered approach by segment. The 30 day time period for landowners and interested persons to file a Statement of Opposition (SOO) was set according to when notice for each segment was completed [Folder 3808419] . The deadlines to file an SOO have now passed for the majority of the route (excluding a small number of lands where service is still outstanding or service may have occurred, but the 30 day period has not yet closed.)

The Commission is now determining next steps for detailed route proceedings. The following tables provide an overview of the status of detailed route (PPBoR) approvals and detailed route hearings. Information will be updated as the Commission issues decisions and approvals.
