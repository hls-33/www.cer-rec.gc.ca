Oral hearing session: to collect, and allow questioning of, oral Indigenous traditional evidence

20 November to 6 December 2018

Trans Mountain files reply evidence

  • Reply evidence must be served on all intervenors

11 December 2018
(formerly 26 November 2018)

All Parties file information requests regarding other Parties’ evidence

  • Information requests must be relevant to one or more of the issues identified in Appendix 1, and also to the evidence of the Party to which the questions are asked
  • Information requests must be served on all other Parties

17 December 2018
(formerly 3 December 2018)

All Parties respond to information requests asked of them

  • Responses to information requests must be served on all other Parties

31 December 2018
(formerly 13 December 2018)

Motion Day – As considered necessary, Parties file notices of motion on the adequacy of other Parties’ responses to their own information requests (“motions to compel”)

  • Motions to compel must be served on all other Parties

4 January 2019
(formerly 17 December 2018)

Parties that have motions to compel directed at them may respond to those motions to compel

8 January 2019

Parties that filed motions to compel, and had responses provided by the other Party, may reply to those responses
