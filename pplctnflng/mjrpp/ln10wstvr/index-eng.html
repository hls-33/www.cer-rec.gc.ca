Enbridge Pipelines Inc.

On January 26,  2017, the NEB approved Enbridge's Line 10 Westover Segment Replacement Project.

  • Contacts

Project Information

  • Enbridge proposes to replace 32 kilometres (km) of existing 12 inch-diameter Line 10 pipe with 35 km of new 20 inch-diameter pipe in southern Ontario. It would connect Enbridge’s Westover Terminal to its Nanticoke Junction Facility in the City of Hamilton. The Project includes three new sections for proposed reroutes from the existing right-of-way totaling approximately 11 km.
  • Enbridge applied [Filing A74506] and [Filing A74508] under Section 58 of the NEB Act for approval to construct and operate this section of the replacement Line 10 pipeline, and under Section 45.1 of the National Energy Board Onshore Pipeline Regulations to decommission this section of the Line 10 pipeline that is being replaced.

Support for Participants

Process Advisor

The main role of a Process Advisor is to provide information to the general public and support to participants in the hearing. They can also answer questions about the NEB’s process.

  • Process Fact Sheet [Document A4Y2A7]

You can contact a Process Advisor by calling 1-800-899-1265 (toll-free) or emailing L10RP.ProcessHelp@cer-rec.gc.ca.

Application to Participate

Attention

Email is not a valid method of filing regulatory documents with the NEB. Comments received by email will not be considered. You must apply to participate.

The Application to Participate (ATP) process was held from 22 February to 14 March 2016. The process is now closed.

The Board will decide if late applications will be accepted and whether you will be allowed to participate. Please contact the Process Advisor to discuss the filing process for a late application to participate.
