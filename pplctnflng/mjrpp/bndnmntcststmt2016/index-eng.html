Project Information

The Board is reviewing the abandonment cost estimates (ACE) for all regulated pipeline companies.

In the MH-001-2012 ACE proceeding [Folder 782061], the Board heard from Group 1 companies that they were committed to advancing studies and research on the physical issues of pipeline abandonment. As new technologies and best practices continue to evolve, there would be opportunities to learn and revise the estimates accordingly. Additionally, the Board noted that evidence justifying a different assumption could be brought forward during the regular review of the cost estimates and reiterated that cost estimates would be regularly reviewed at least every five years to help mitigate the under or over collection of funds.

Group 1 companies have submitted their revised ACE and supporting documents.
