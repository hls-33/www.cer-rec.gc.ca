The Canada Energy Regulator Library provides access to CER, industry, and energy inf ormation. The CER Library collection is unique and some materials can only be accessed through our Library. The Library takes great pride in collecting and preserving the literature of the CER. Not only do we hold a full collection of all hearing materials (exhibits, hearing transcripts, and Reasons for Decision), but we also have a complete collection of CER reports, studies, and research papers.

The main collection consists of statutes, federal regulations, decision documents from Canada Energy Regulatorother jurisdictions, books, annual reports, studies, reports, speeches and standards. The remainder of the Library collection consists of directories, dictionaries, encyclopedias and industry-related indexes in the reference collection; documentaries and instructional videos in the audio visual collection; as well as oil, gas and electricity magazines and journals.

The Library makes available to the public a range of services including consultation of regulatory documents, copies of CER publications, as well as referrals to internal and external sources of information. While members of the public cannot borrow Library materials directly from the CER Library, many public, university, and corporate libraries can borrow material on an individual’s behalf through our inter-library loan service.

Personal Information Collection Statement

All personal information created, held or collected by the Canada Energy Regulator is protected under the Privacy Act. This means that you will be informed of the purpose for which it is being collected and how to exercise your right of access to that information. You will be asked for your consent where appropriate.

Public Communications
