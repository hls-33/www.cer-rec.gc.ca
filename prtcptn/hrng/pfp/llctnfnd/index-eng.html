Participant Funding Program Quick Links

Program Guide Direct Deposit [PDF] Claim Form [PDF] Participant Funding Reports

If you have any questions, please email PFP.PAFP@cer-rec.gc.ca or call 1-800-899-1265 (toll-free).

Awards made under the Participant Funding Program are announced on the CER website, here and under the proactive disclosure of contribution. This means that the information provided in a PFP Funding Application may be made public if you are a successful applicant. Note: a PFP project report is only created once all awards have been made on the project.
