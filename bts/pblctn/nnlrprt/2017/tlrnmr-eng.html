To Learn More about the NEB

There is much more to say about the performance of the NEB over the past year, and we would encourage you to review the web-based program information available on our website. We have made great strides towards having much more of our data and information available on-line, including ensuring we are demonstrating a strong commitment to the Government of Canada’s Open Data initiative – where Canadians can review, download and use the NEB’s data for your own analysis and purposes.
