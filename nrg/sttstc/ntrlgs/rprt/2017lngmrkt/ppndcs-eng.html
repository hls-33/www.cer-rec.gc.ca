Appendix 2: U.S. Regasification Projects

In the years prior to the large-scale accessibility of shale and tight gas resources, the U.S. invested in the construction of LNG import terminals, anticipating a need to import foreign natural gas to meet future demand. However, in the wake of this growth in shale development, the U.S. decommissioned, stalled, or canceled many import terminal projects.
