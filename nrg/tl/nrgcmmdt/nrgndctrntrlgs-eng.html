Energy Commodity Indicators – Natural Gas

This dashboard presents a graphical overview of key indicators for natural gas markets in Canada. Sources for this data include Statistics Canada, the National Energy Board, Government of Alberta, U.S. Energy Information Administration and Baker Hughes. The main indicators for this dashboard are:

  • Natural gas prices
  • Marketable natural gas production
  • Natural gas demand
  • Natural gas exports
  • Natural gas imports
  • Imports of liquefied natural gas
  • Natural gas-directed rig count
  • Natural gas supply and demand projections to 2040

The units in this dashboard are available in cubic metres or cubic feet, and in a monthly or daily aggregation. Select unit on the upper left hand side of the dashboard. This dashboard utilizes a series of interactive filters. To remove the filter, click on the previously selected element or refresh the web page. Where applicable, totals for each dataset are available in the hover tooltip. Links to downloadable datasets are available at the bottom of this page.

The dashboard is updated with any available new data on a weekly basis. Please send feedback on this dashboard to energy.markets@cer-rec.gc.ca.

  • Imports of Liquefied Natural Gas – National Energy Board, Commodity Statistics (Natural Gas Imports – Historical Chart of Volumes);

  • Canadian Natural Gas Supply and Demand Projection to 2040 – Canada's Energy Future 2018, National Energy Board, (2018 report Figure Data).
