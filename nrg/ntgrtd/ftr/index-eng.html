Canada's Energy Future

Send Us Your Comments

We want to hear your thoughts on the Energy Futures Report. Whether it is a response to the outcomes in the Report or your thoughts on the Web site, we want to hear from you. E-mail us at energyfutures@cer-rec.gc.ca.

Long-term analyses are required to ensure that oil and gas exports do not exceed the surplus remaining after Canada’s reasonably foreseeable energy needs are met in the long term.

In addition to conducting its own quantitative analysis, the CER consults with Canadian energy experts and interested stakeholders representing industry, government, non-governmental organizations and academia in developing Canada’s Energy Future reports.
