Share your ideas!

The Canada Energy Regulator (CER) regulates pipelines, energy development and trade in the Canadian public interest. By sharing your ideas, you help us better understand the diversity of opinions of Canadians and ensure we protect what is most important to you.

This page focuses on consultation and engagement activities that fall outside of the CER hearing processes.

If you are interested in participating in a project specific review, please visit the Major Applications and Projects web page or the Apply to participate web page.
