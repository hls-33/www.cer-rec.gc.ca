Canadian Energy Data Links

This database is a compilation of Canadian energy data links. The tabs were organized to follow an energy matrix logic – from resource to final consumption. There are two streams: the “oil and gas pathway” and the “electricity pathway”. This is our first attempt at bringing this information together. Should you notice that we missed a link or that one of the links is broken, please let us know. You can reach us at canadaenergydatalinks@cer-rec.gc.ca.

The pathway approach considers oil and gas from its source through to end use along with the price at various stages. Links to the information may be comprehensive or by the individual elements that comprise the energy system.
